Payment of registration fee:

Registration fee is 2000 RMB (300 USD) for the participants and 1500 RMB (220 USD) for the accompanying person, which covers access to all workshop sessions, the lunches and coffee breaks specified in the conference program, conference materials, the welcome reception and the conference dinner.

In addition, for those who wish to attend the two-day excursion (Jun 13-14) to the Huangshan (Yellow Mountain) after the conference, we will charge extra 1400 RMB (200 USD) to cover the communication, tickets, food and one-night accommodation. The excursion ends at about 15:00, Jun 14 , at which time we will provide a shuttle service to the Huangshan North high-speed railway station. Then the participants can make it to Hangzhou or Shanghai Hongqiao before 19:00 and to Shanghai Pudong before 21:00. For those who wish to spend another night at the excursion site, we can help to book the accommodation.
